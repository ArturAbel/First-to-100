Game Instructions Roll the dice at least once during your turn and accumulate the result. You can choose to roll again or click "hold" to save the current points and end your turn. If you roll a double six (6-6), you lose the points accumulated in that turn and your turn ends. The player who reaches the exact points limit wins. If a player exceeds the points limit, they lose.
